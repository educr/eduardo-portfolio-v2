## Overview
My team collaborated with nutritionists to create a visual instruction system designed to reduce medication abandonment through clear, illustrated guidance. I participated in workshops and designed visual instructions that translated complex medication steps into simple, patient-friendly visuals supporting adherence.
![Hero](/cases/pert/hero.png)


## Team

Cross-departmental collaboration between Pathfinders (within UX Design) and MSK Nutrition. 

## My contributions
Workshop, stakeholder discussions, design and illustration of visual instructions.
